FAQ

  • Do I need KotOR / TSL? Yes. Holowan Browser Runtime replaces the program, not the content. You must legally own the PC game and supply its files yourself.
  • Do you distribute game data? No. We do not host art, archives, audio, or other copyrighted game assets. We do not condone piracy.
  • Affiliated with Lucasfilm / BioWare / Obsidian / Aspyr? No.
  • Which browser? Desktop Chrome or Edge for the directory picker used by Holowan Browser Runtime.
  • Tor? No onion is published. Treat any claimed .onion as unverified.
  • Need to buy the game? See Need KotOR? on the home page.
  • Private proprietary research? Holowan does not distribute proprietary dumps or private research trees. Do not request them.
